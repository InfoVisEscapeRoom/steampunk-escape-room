About Quest

A secret workshop hidden in brass and shadow.

Our guest room recreates a secretive space inside a remote Admozur escenrepit cant, where marketralogy escapists experience rare, visceral mysteries.

The Forgotten Inventor’s Lab

Your team unravels the reverberating intent shrouded in valves and glowing vopceriums. Decode the revolving, ornate apparatus to illuminate impossible answers.

Puzzle Structure

Mechanical challenges await at every turn.

Each puzzle is designed to test your team's logic, creativity, and collaboration. From intricate clockwork mechanisms to complex pressure systems, every challenge tells a part of the lab's mysterious story.

Progressive Difficulty

Start with simpler mechanisms to understand the lab's mechanics, then advance to more complex puzzles that require combining multiple solutions to unlock the final mystery.

All mechanisms are handmade using traditional craftsmanship.
