About Quest

A laboratory abandoned during the snail-virus outbreak

This escape room places your team inside a deserted research facility near the epicenter of a dangerous virus that transforms people into snails. The last scientists working here were close to a breakthrough—but disappeared before finishing their cure.

The Mission Briefing

As elite specialists arriving by zeppelin, you must enter the lab, search through the remaining research, and uncover the scientists’ final discoveries. With only one hour and no support from the outside crew, your task is simple but critical: work through every station, combine your expertise, and find the cure—before the virus finds you.

Puzzle Structure

A multi-stage mission built for teamwork and expertise

The escape room features 5 main puzzle stations, room-based challenges, and a final combined puzzle. All 5 student groups — doctors, physicists, chemists, malacologists, and geographics — must complete every station to collect the clues required for the final task.

Collaborative Progression

Each station contains unique data for every team. Groups must analyse and interpret their own specialized information using their clipboards and keycards to activate puzzles. Stations can be reset and solved repeatedly without assistance, and no single solution reveals the answers for another group. Finishing early unlocks extra room puzzles that provide additional clues before the final challenge.

Making Of

Crafted with 3D-printed precision and steampunk design

Every puzzle station in the escape room is built using custom 3D-printed mechanisms, engineered to match the brass-and-steam aesthetic of the abandoned laboratory. Each element — from interactive components to the atmospheric props — is designed to support the story of the snail-virus outbreak and the five expert teams sent to uncover the cure.
